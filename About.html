About the Game

The game mechanics, maps, sprites, animations and weapons will be based off CS:GO (de_dust 2 in particular). Since I am limited to 2D, I will only be able to recreate particular areas of the maps (e.g. A long, Mid, Cat). I will also try to implement grenades if possible.
